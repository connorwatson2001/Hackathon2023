Welcome to QuickTag

Home organization, reimagined.
Never lose anything again.

A household-oriented
inventory management system
that's ACTUALLY user-friendly.

All it takes is a few simple steps!

Stick your QuickTags

Attach your QuickTags to whatever storage system you use. The QuickTags will stick to any flat surface! And move your QuickTags as many times as you want- they will still stick!

Scan your QuickTags

Scan each QuickTag in our app. Then you can give each location a name and add your items to each location. Don't worry- you can add or remove items as much as you want!

Never lose anything again

Next time (and every time) that you need to find something, don't dig through boxes! Search for your item in the app or just view your items by location!
